What is Meditation?

Meditation is a set of techniques that are intended to encourage a heightened state of awareness and focused attention. Meditation is also a consciousness technique that has been shown to have a wide number of benefits on psychological well-being.

#1 Deep Breathing




  • Find a comfortable upright seated position.
  • Inhale a deep breath through the nose so that it reaches the bottom of the lungs and the stomach expands.
  • Continue inhaling until the top of your lungs are filled with air, your chest and rib cage should be expanded.
  • Hold the breath for 2-3 seconds
  • Finally, slowly exhale the breath out of your mouth, removing all breath.
  • Repeat this pattern for 5-10 minutes
