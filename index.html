What is Meditation?

Meditation is the practice of techniques that strenghten the indivduals state of awareness and focus of attention, to factors such as breathing. Daily practice of meditation has many benefits including relieving stress, improving sleep quality, increased attention, improved memory and mental clarity.

Exercises

Focus Meditation

Focused meditation requires you to focus your attention without being distracted by your thoughts. You can focus on something visual like a statue. Many focus their attention on audiotory, like a recording of ocean waves, something constant like your breathing or your heart beathing.

Many people find it easier to do this than focus on nothing, but the concept is the same - staying in the present moment, circumventing the constant stream of commentart from your conscious mind, and allowing yourself to slip into an altered state of conscious.

Activity Based Meditation

Mindfulnesss meditation similar to Activity Based Meditation, might not look like your idea of meditation. Mindfulness simply involves staying in the present rather than thinking about the future or the past. Again, this can be more difficult than it seems. Focusing on sensations you feel in your body is one way to stay "in the now". Focusing on emotions and where you feel them in your body - not examining why you feel them, but just experiencing them as sensations - is another
